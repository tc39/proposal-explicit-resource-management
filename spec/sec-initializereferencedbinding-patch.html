InitializeReferencedBinding ( _V_, _W_, _hint_ )

The abstract operation InitializeReferencedBinding takes arguments _V_ and _W_, _W_, and _hint_. It performs the following steps when called:

1. ReturnIfAbrupt(_V_). 1. ReturnIfAbrupt(_W_). 1. Assert: _V_ is a Reference Record. 1. Assert: IsUnresolvableReference(_V_) is *false*. 1. Assert: _hint_ is ~normal~, ~sync~, or ~async~. 1. Let _base_ be _V_.[[Base]]. 1. Assert: _base_ is an Environment Record. 1. Return _base_.InitializeBinding(_V_.[[ReferencedName]], _W_, _hint_).
