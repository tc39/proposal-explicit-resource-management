Well-Known Symbols

Specification Name [[Description]] Value and Purpose
@@asyncDispose `"Symbol.asyncDispose"` A method that performs explicit resource cleanup on an object. Called by the semantics of the `using await const` and `using await value` statements while in an async function, async generator, or the top level of a _Module_.
@@dispose `"Symbol.dispose"` A method that performs explicit resource cleanup on an object. Called by the semantics of the `using const` and `using value` statements.
